Top 3 Tips

  1. Hydrate - Colorado has an elevation of almost a mile, so it's important to drink a lot of water as you can get dehydrated quickly. You can also prevent elevation sickness this way
  2. Bring Hiking Shoes - Colorado is known for its natural beaty so make sure you are dressed accordingly to be able to see it. Make sure to bring a pair of hiking boots ideally or sneakers
  3. Rent a Car - While Denver and Boulder have great public transportation, if you are looking to travel around national parks and get to other places of the state like Golden, travel by car is best. Look for rentals or find a friend that has a car!
