California - The Golden State

California was the second trip got to take this summer. Growing up on the East Coast I had always dreamed about going to the West Coast to visit California and see what was on the other side. When my Dad offered me to join him on a business trip there I jumped at the opportunity and the business trip turned into an exploration of the Golden State!

Highlights from My Trip

  • The artists village in Balboa Park is a must-see unique attraction of the city
  • Climbing Coit Tower in downtown SFO gives you spectacular views of the skyline
  • Muir Woods National Forest is a indescribable experience getting to feel the size of Redwood Treets

Who I Travelled With

I was able to join my Dad on this trip. Originally just a business trip for a conference in San Diego, it quicly morphed into a road trip and adventure around California. On the way were were also able to meet up with family members in San Jose

Top 3 Tips

  1. Utilize Public Transit - Both SFO and San Diego have excellent public transit systems and if you plan on flying SFO BART will take you right from the airport to downtown
  2. Bring Layers - While San Diego can be relatively warm, SFO can be rather unpredictable with the temperature so it's best to bring an extra layer to take on and off as needed
  3. Make Reservations - While many attractions in SFO can be free or relatively affordable many require reservations like spots at national parks or even time slots at Coit Tower
